Description

Here's a classic, moist banana bread recipe that's easy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup (75g) melted butter
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar (use less for a less sweet loaf)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (190g) all-purpose flour

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Stir in the melted butter.
  4. Mix in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla.
  5. Sprinkle the baking soda and salt over the mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Add the flour and stir just until incorporated (don't overmix).
  7. Fold in any optional add-ins if using.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  9. Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
