Quinoa Salad

This is some delicious tasty goodness and I love it.

  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 1 block Feta
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1/2 Red Onion
  • 1 whole Cucumber
  • 1 Can Chickpeas
  • 1 Lemon
  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  1. Add quinoa to 1 1/2 cup water in pot and bring to boil
  2. Let quinoa simmer at low heat for 15min
  3. When finished let quinoa cool
  4. Set lemon and Olive oil to side
  5. Chop remaining ingredients to preferred size
  6. Add everything to salad bowl
  7. Squeeze one whole lemon to quinoa
  8. Add Olive oil
  9. Mix thoroughly
  10. ENJOY!
