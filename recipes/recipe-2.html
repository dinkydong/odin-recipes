Wings

Spicy delicious so goodness

  • 1 lb Wings
  • 1/2 cup Franks Hot Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 6 cloves Garlic
  • 1 Ginger Root
  1. Mince Garlic
  2. Grate 2tbsp Ginger
  3. Add all ingredients to bag and let marinate for 30min
  4. Preheat oven to 375C
  5. Add wings to oven and cook for 25min
  6. ENJOY!
