Description

Here's a refined yet approachable Beef Tartare recipe—elegant, flavorful, and great for impressing.

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) beef tenderloin or sirloin, very finely diced (super fresh!)
  • 1 egg yolk (very fresh, or use pasteurized)
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp finely chopped shallots
  • 1 tbsp capers, rinsed and chopped
  • 1 tbsp cornichons, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • A few dashes of Tabasco (to taste)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt & freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley or chives (optional)

    • Optional Garnishes:

  • Microgreens
  • Shaved parmesan
  • Toasted baguette slices or crackers

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prep the beef:

    2. Freeze the beef for 10–15 minutes to firm it up slightly (this makes dicing easier). Dice into small cubes using a sharp knife—avoid mincing it too finely.

  2. Make the seasoning base:

    3. In a mixing bowl, combine the mustard, shallots, capers, cornichons, Worcestershire sauce, Tabasco, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

  3. Combine:

    4. Add the diced beef to the bowl and gently fold it together with the seasoning base until well coated.

  4. Plate:

    5. Form into a neat mound or use a ring mold for a cleaner presentation. Carefully place the egg yolk on top.

  5. Garnish and Serve:

    6. Sprinkle with herbs, microgreens, or shaved parmesan if desired. Serve immediately with toast or crackers.
